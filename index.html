Our Production

Indonesia is home to some of the richest biodiversity in the world—including high-quality tropical leaves like betel leaves (daun sirih) and curry leaves (daun kari). IndoLeaves is a newly established sourcing partner, working closely with trusted local farmers across Java, Indonesia. While we are a new player in the global trade, we bring a fresh, transparent, and highly committed approach to the supply chain.

Our products are harvested responsibly, handled with care, and packaged to meet international expectations. We are actively building our export ready processes, including product sorting, food-grade packaging, and logistics readiness.
